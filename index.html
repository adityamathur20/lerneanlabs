The Problem

A compliance burden
that never sleeps.

Every company holding a URN under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (Regulation of Controlled Substances) Order, 2013 carries obligations that run 365 days a year — and the penalty for slipping is severe.

📋

Daily Register. Every Single Day.

Nil-transaction days still require an entry. Saturdays, holidays, slow weeks — the register never pauses. Miss entries and your quarterly return becomes non-compliant.

📅

365 Days. No Exceptions.

The daily register doesn't pause for weekends, slow months, or zero-movement days. Every single day needs an entry. Every single day someone has to do it manually — until now.

■ Weekends still need entries   ■ Working days

⚠️

Three Missed Quarters: URN Revoked

There is no grace period. Three consecutive missed quarterly returns (Form-E or Form-F) and the NCB initiates automatic revocation proceedings — for all registered entities, all locations.
